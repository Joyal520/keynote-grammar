Subject vs. Object Pronouns

A short reference: subject pronouns do the action; object pronouns receive it.

Subject Pronouns (The doers)

Subject pronouns are used as the subject of the verb — they perform the action.

PronounExample Sentence
II like swimming.
WeWe need more time.
YouYou are a genius.
TheyThey were playing in the garden.
HeHe was a lawyer.
SheShe is my friend.
ItIt is a beautiful day.

Object Pronouns (The receivers)

Object pronouns are used as direct/indirect objects or after prepositions — they receive the action.

PronounExample Sentence
meCan you help me?
usThey helped us.
youI will see you tomorrow.
themI saw them on the beach.
himShe invited him to the party.
herDo you know her?
itI have a dog. I give it meat every day.
