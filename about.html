About Keynote Grammar

Keynote Grammar is a creative grammar-learning platform designed by R. J. Roshan to make English learning fun, structured, and practical. Built from the book Keynote Grammar – From Basics to Intermediate, the platform blends lessons, activities, and quizzes into an interactive digital experience. It helps learners build confidence and accuracy step by step — from simple sentences to complex grammar rules.
