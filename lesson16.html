Lesson 16: Simple Future Tense

We use the simple future to talk about actions that will happen in the future.

1. Use

We use the simple future to talk about actions that will happen in the future.

  • I will call you later.
  • She will finish the work tomorrow.
  • They will travel next month.
  • It will rain tonight.
Tip: Use "will" for predictions, promises, instant decisions and offers.

2. Formulation

Subject + will + base verb
