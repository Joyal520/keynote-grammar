Simple Past — Questions & Negatives

Audio Explanation

1. Forming Questions in the Simple Past

Use:

Did + subject + base form

Examples

  • Did you watch a movie last night?
  • Did they visit the museum?
  • Did she bake a cake?
  • Did he repair his car yesterday afternoon?

2. Forming Negatives in the Simple Past

Use:

Subject + did not + base form

Examples

  • She did not bake a cake.
  • He did not repair his car.
  • We did not watch a movie.
  • They did not visit the museum.

3. Summary

Form Structure Examples
Questions Did + subject + base form Did she bake a cake?
Did he repair his car?
Negatives Subject + did not + base form She did not bake a cake.
He did not repair his car.
