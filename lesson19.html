Adverbs

An adverb modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb. Learn formation, types and examples.

1. What is an Adverb?

An adverb is a word that modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb.

  • Modify a verb: Snails move slowly.
  • Modify an adjective: The movie was very interesting.
  • Modify another adverb: He drove too quickly.
